We started Robin Home Care because we couldn't find the service we wanted for our own relatives. Almost a decade on, that's still the benchmark.

Our story

From one family's frustration to Ealing's most trusted home care team.

In 2016, we went looking for home care for an elderly relative. We found plenty of agencies, plenty of promises, and very little consistency. Different faces every visit. Rushed thirty-minute slots. Care plans that read more like contracts than conversations.

So we built the alternative. Small, stable teams. Proper training. Care coordinators who answer their own phones. A business that puts the home – and the person in it – before the rota.

We're still independent, still family-run, and still based in Ealing. Our CQC Outstanding rating confirms what our families already know, and we're proud of it. But the real measure is whether the person we care for is having a better week. That's the one that matters.

– Ivan, Founder

What we stand for

Four principles that shape every care visit.

These aren't values hung on a wall. They're the tests we apply to every rota, every carer, and every conversation with a family.

I.

Dignity, always

How care is delivered matters as much as what is delivered. The small courtesies – knocking before entering, asking before acting – aren't optional.

II.

Continuity over convenience

We build small teams around each client so trust has room to grow. We'd rather turn work away than break that promise.

III.

Honesty about what we can't do

If we're not the right fit, we'll say so and help you find someone who is. No aggressive sales, no mission creep.

IV.

The person first

Needs are individual. So are hobbies, preferences, and the way someone likes their tea. We pay attention to all of it.
