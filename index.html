Our promise

What you can count on from Robin Home Care.

Care should feel steady. These are the commitments we make to every family, written plainly and meant seriously.

01

The same faces, week to week.

We build small, consistent teams around each client so trust has room to grow. No agency revolving door.

02

A named care coordinator.

One person you can call by first name, who knows your family's situation and can answer straight away.

Carers who've been properly trained.

Our story

A West London care service, built the way we'd want it for our own families.

Robin Home Care began in 2016 with a simple frustration: finding reliable, warm, properly-trained home care for our own relatives was far harder than it should have been. So we built the service we'd been looking for.

Nearly a decade on, we're still family-run, still Ealing-based, and still turning up for the people who've trusted us from the start. Our CQC Outstanding rating matters because it reflects what our clients already tell us – but it's the quiet feedback in a kitchen that tells us we're doing the job right.
